$$\delta_i=\delta_1\cdot b^{i-1}$$ $$t_{ges}=\sum^\infty_{i=1}\delta_i=\sum^\infty_{i=1}\delta_1\cdot b^{i-1}=\delta_1\frac{1}{1-b}$$ $$t_n=\sum^n_{i=1}\delta_i=\sum^\infty_{i=1}\delta_i-\sum^\infty_{i=n+1}\delta_i=t_{ges}-\delta_1\sum^\infty_{i=n+1}b^{i-1}=t_{ges}-\delta_1\sum^\infty_{i=1}b^{n+i-1}$$ $$=t_{ges}-\delta_1\cdot b^n\sum^\infty_{i=1}b^{i-1}=t_{ges}-t_{ges}\cdot b^n=t_{ges}(1-b^n)$$ $$\Leftrightarrow\frac{t_n}{t_{ges}}=1-b^n\Leftrightarrow n=\frac{\log\left(1-\frac{t_n}{t_{ges}}\right)}{\log b}$$
